Who we are?

We are a team of four passionate female developers who came together during our graduation internship at the Information Technology Institute (ITI), specializing in Angular frontend development. Our project reflects both our technical growth and creative spirit, as we aimed to build a responsive, user-friendly web application that showcases handcrafted products in an elegant and accessible way.

The idea behind our project, "Handicrafts," is to create a digital platform that supports and promotes local artisans and handmade product sellers.
